About MS Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, widely known as MS Dhoni, is one of the most successful and beloved cricketers of all time. As the former captain of the Indian national team, Dhoni led India to victory in major tournaments, including the ICC World Cup 2007 (T20), ICC World Cup 2011 (ODI), and ICC Champions Trophy 2013. His leadership, calm demeanor, and sharp cricketing acumen have made him an iconic figure in world cricket.
